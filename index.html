About Me


Hello, I am Charles Dahill but everybody calls me "Chay" (it's pronounced the same as Che Guevara). I am currently transitioning into a career in computer programming. I am finishing up a certificate program in full stack development where I have been focusing on javascript, node.js, express.js, react.js, mysql, and mongo. I have also been using udemy courses to teach myself other languages such as php, Java, and C#. I love a challenge and have been enjoying the mental stimulation that I get from programming. As well as programming, I thouroughly enjoy working with computer hardware; I build my own systems and often do repairs for friends and family. Before I decided to become a computer programmer, I spent the past five years as a high school science teacher in Phoenix, Arizona. The subjects I taught were Earth Science, Physics, and introductory Chemistry. My education degrees include:

  • Certificate in full stack development, University of Arizona
  • M.Ed in Secondary Education, University of Arizona
  • M.A in Museum Studies, New York University
  • B.A in Physics with a minor in Astronomy, University of Arizona
